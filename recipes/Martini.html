Martini

The martini is a classic cocktail at bars across the world and often customized. Below is the classic recipe for this cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 oz Gin of choice
  • 0.5 oz dry vermouth
  • Ice
  • Lemon twist or olive for garnish

Steps

  1. Ice the martini glass to chill
  2. Pour ice into mixing beaker
  3. Add gin and vermouth to the beaker
  4. Stir ingredients until the beaker is cold at the bottom or begins to frost
  5. Empty the martini glass and double strain from the beaker into the glass
  6. Place garnish either lemon twist or olive inside the glass
