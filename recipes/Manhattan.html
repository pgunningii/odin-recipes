Manhattan

The Manhattan is a classic cocktail who's recipe actually comes from the area code for Manhattan island in New York. It is a stout drink consisting of almost all liquor.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Bourbon or Rye
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes aromatic bitters
  • 1 luxardo cherry for garnish
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Pour ice into mixing beaker
  2. pour bourbon/rye, vermouth, and bitters into the beaker
  3. stir until beaker is cold or frost begins to form
  4. double strain from the beaker into a coupe glass unless requested on the rocks, then pour over ice
  5. garnish with one luxardo cherry
