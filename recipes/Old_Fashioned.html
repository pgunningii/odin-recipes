Old Fashioned

The old fashioned is a classic cocktail and a standard at any bar. The perfect combination for a whiskey drink with just the right amount of sweetness.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye
  • 2 dashes aromatic bitters
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 1 orange peel as garnish
  • 1 luxardo cherry as garnish
  • 1 large ice cube

Steps

  1. Place ice cube in whiskey glass
  2. Pour 2 oz boubon/rye over ice cube
  3. Dash aromatic bitters twice into the glass
  4. Pour 1/2 oz of simple syrup into the glass
  5. Stir for 15 revolutions
  6. Express orange peel over the rim of the glass
  7. Place luxardo cherry on the cube
